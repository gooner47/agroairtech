Centrifugal and Axial Fans

At Agro AirTech, we manufacture robust, high-efficiency centrifugal and axial flow fans for a wide range of industrial uses.

  • Centrifugal fans are ideal for bag filters, ESPs, dust collectors, and furnace combustion systems, delivering stable airflow under high static pressure and heavy particulate loads.

    • They're also used in ventilation systems, dryers, kilns, and material handling setups.

  • Axial flow fans are engineered for high-volume, low-pressure applications such as paddy dryers, cooling towers, tunnel ventilation, and general exhaust systems.

Each fan is tailored to suit your specific airflow, pressure, and temperature requirements — available in various materials, impeller designs, and mounting styles.

Centrifugal and Axial Fans

ID (Induced Draft) Fans for Bag Filter, ESP, and Boilers

Heavy-duty centrifugal fans designed to handle hot, dust-laden flue gases from boilers, bag filters, and ESPs, creating negative pressure for efficient gas movement in cement, steel, and power plants.

FD (Forced Draft) Fans for Boilers and Kilns

High-efficiency centrifugal fans that supply combustion air under positive pressure to furnaces, kilns, and boilers, ensuring optimal air-fuel mixture in thermal power, cement, and process industries.

Air Slide Blowers / High Pressure Blowers

High-static pressure centrifugal blowers engineered for pneumatic conveying, air slides, and fluidization systems across cement plants, boiler houses, and bulk material handling units, delivering consistent performance in resistance-heavy applications.

DIDW Blowers (Double Inlet, Double Width)

Centrifugal blowers with dual inlets and impellers spanning full wheel width for balanced airflow and higher volume capacity at low to medium pressures — ideal for HVAC systems, air handling units, dust extraction, and process ventilation in large industrial setups.

SISW Blowers (Single Inlet, Single Width)

Compact centrifugal blowers with single-side suction and narrower wheel profile, engineered for high static pressure applications such as bag filters, boiler ID fans, and localized ventilation in cement, steel, and material handling industries.

Axial Flow Fans (or Tube Axial Fans)

Designed for high-volume airflow at low to moderate static pressure, axial flow fans are ideal for dryers, cooling towers, tunnel ventilation, and general exhaust in cement plants, rice mills, and process industries.
