Who we are

At Agro AirTech, we specialize in ISO-compliant, high-precision dynamic balancing services for rotating equipment across industries.

Whether it's a small impeller or a 7-ton armature, our team ensures every component leaves our workshop with minimal vibration and maximum operational life.

We specialize in ISO-compliant, high-precision dynamic balancing.

What we do

We offer off-site dynamic balancing and on-site balancing & vibration analysis for:

  • Impellers (Centrifugal/Axial)
  • Pump Rotors & Shafts
  • Motor Armatures
  • Flywheels, Pulleys & Rollers
  • Crusher and Grinder Shafts
  • Generator & Turbine Rotors

From preventive maintenance to post-repair balancing, we provide complete balancing solutions for critical equipment.

Our infrastructure

We have the following dynamic balancing machines and analyzer:

  • 1-Ton capacity balancing machines: max dia. 1600 mm
  • 7-Ton capacity balancing machine: max dia. 3250 mm
  • In-situ balancing machine.
  • Vibration analyzer

Each machine is calibrated and maintained to meet ISO standards, ensuring consistently reliable results across all balancing jobs.

If you manufacture or maintain rotating equipment — we’re the partner you can trust.
