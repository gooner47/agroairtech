Who we are

At Agro AirTech, we manufacture heavy-duty centrifugal and axial fans, air handling systems, air pollution control equipment, and components like impellers and dampers.

We serve core sectors including cement, steel, power, and process industries, with products built to perform in high-temperature, abrasive, and corrosive environments.

In addition to manufacturing, we offer high-precision dynamic balancing — both on-site and in-house — using 1-ton and 7-ton capacity machines.

Our goal is simple: to deliver reliable, application-specific solutions backed by sound engineering and prompt support.

Specialists in industrial fan engineering and dynamic balancing — delivering reliable components for high-temperature, dust-laden, and process-critical applications.

What we do

We design, build, and support high-performance airflow systems:

  • Centrifugal and axial fans for high-temp, abrasive, and corrosive applications
  • Air pollution control equipment like bag filters, cyclones and dust collectors
  • Impellers and blades for specific airflow, pressure, and material requirements
  • Butterfly, multi-louver, and variable vane dampers
  • Dynamic balancing services for impellers, rotors, crusher shafts, etc.
  • On-site balancing and vibration diagnosis by trained technicians
  • ISO-compliant run testing and calibrated instruments for quality assurance

Complete airflow solutions — fans, impellers, accessories, air pollution control equipment and precision balancing supported by ISO-compliant infrastructure.

Our infrastructure

We have the following infrastructure to ensure quality work and quick turnaround:

  • Dedicated facilities for fan and equipment manufacturing and assembly, and component inspection
  • Experienced technicians and a process-driven approach for consistent quality across builds and services
  • Dynamic balancing machines (up to 7 ton capacity and 3350 mm dia) for impellers, armature rotors, crusher shafts, etc.
  • Calibrated testing instruments: vibration meters, anemometers, pressure gauges, tachometers, and more
  • ISO-compliant testing facilities for performance run tests and quality checks

Each machine is calibrated regularly and maintained to meet ISO standards, ensuring smooth and efficient performance.
