About
our company

Agro AirTech manufactures industrial centrifugal fans and air pollution control equipment like ID fans, axial flow fans, and blowers for ESPs and bag filters. We serve industries such as cement, steel, ferro alloys, and rice mills, delivering reliable airflow and dust control solutions.

We also provide ISO-compliant dynamic balancing services for rotating equipment. Our advanced machines and experienced technicians help reduce vibration, minimize downtime, and extend equipment life.
