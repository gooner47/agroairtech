Spares and Accessories

At Agro AirTech, we offer essential fan components and flow control accessories designed for durability, compatibility, and performance across a range of industrial environments. Our key offerings include:

  • Impellers – Dynamically balanced impellers for ID, FD, and process blowers.
  • Axial fan blades – Durable blades in aluminum or FRP for high-volume axial flow fans.
  • Variable vane dampers – Precision airflow control with external linkages and low pressure drop.
  • ML dampers – Multi-louvre dampers for effective isolation or throttling in duct systems.
  • Butterfly dampers – Compact shut-off dampers ideal for flue gas and clean air lines.
  • Inlet boxes – Streamlined airflow entry for improved fan performance.

Each part is built to integrate seamlessly with our systems or as a retrofit to other standard industrial setups.

Built for high-temperature, abrasive, and corrosive environments — crafted in MS, SS, or aluminum for lasting durability.

Spares and accessories for fans

ID, FD, process blower Impellers

Axial fan impellers

Axial fan blades built for high-volume air movement in hot, humid, or corrosive environments, using cast aluminum or FRP for durability and efficiency.

Inlet box

Designed for uniform airflow distribution and reduced turbulence at fan inlets, ideal for enhancing performance in ID/FD fans used in thermal and process industries.

Multi-louvre dampers
