Air pollution control
equipments

Agro AirTech designs and manufactures robust air pollution control systems tailored for demanding industrial applications. Our offerings include:

  • Bag filter units for fine particulate filtration in boilers, furnaces, and cement kilns.
  • Cyclones and Multi-Cyclones for pre-cleaning coarse dust in crusher houses, clinker plants, and raw material handling zones.
  • Dust Collectors for localized extraction at belt transfers, packing stations, and material bins.
  • Rotary Air Lock Valves (RAVs) to facilitate controlled discharge of collected dust from hoppers and silos.

Built for longevity and performance, our systems ensure cleaner emissions, regulatory compliance, and improved process efficiency across cement, steel, ferro alloy, rice milling, and foundry industries.

Air pollution control equipments

Pulse Jet Bag Filter Unit

Engineered for continuous duty in cement kilns, steel furnaces, boilers, and material handling lines requiring high-efficiency dust collection under high-temperature, high-dust load conditions.

Dust Collectors

Engineered for continuous duty in crushing plants, grinding units, silo top ventilation, and bulk material transfer points requiring reliable particulate extraction under moderate dust loads and airflow variability.

Cyclones

Heavy-duty pre-separators for coarse dust in cement mills, boiler lines, and process exhausts — ideal for reducing dust load before bag filters or ID fans in high-abrasion environments.

Rotary Air Lock Valves (RAVs)

Dust-tight rotary feeders for controlled discharge from bag filters and cyclones, maintaining system pressure in cement, steel, and bulk material handling applications.
